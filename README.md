# 💫 About Me:

Most technology platforms don't fail because of bad engineers.

They fail because no one could translate the business into something engineers could actually build.

I've spent 6+ years being the person who closes that gap.

I started in B2B enterprise IT at Wipro, working as a Client Service Business Analyst across large-scale technology projects, learning how to gather requirements, drive cross-functional delivery and turn stakeholder chaos into structured execution.

Then I moved into FinTech.

At Broadridge Financial Services, one of the world's largest capital markets infrastructure firms, I spent nearly 4 years embedded in high-stakes, EU-regulated platforms serving JP Morgan, HSBC, Deutsche Bank, RBC, Société Générale, Mizuho, Wells Fargo, SMBC, IG, LSEG and others.

Today, I am part of the core team at Babylon in Dublin, a multicultural integration platform connecting Ireland's diverse communities through a web and mobile app, radio shows and live events. As Project Manager, I lead a team of 9 directly, overseeing Agile delivery across engineering, product and business teams as we work toward a full product launch in the coming months, reporting directly to the Founder.

I wasn't just supporting delivery.

I was owning it, across business analysis, QA, project coordination and product operations.

That's where I stopped thinking like a BA and started thinking like a product builder.

What that's delivered across both environments:

→ 30% improvement in operational execution and delivery quality (Broadridge)

→ 25–30% reduction in post-release defects through QA integration from day one

→ 34% faster stakeholder reporting through Power BI dashboards built for decisions, not vanity metrics (Wipro)

→ 20% boost in operational efficiency at Babylon through Agile delivery coordination and structured reporting

Tools I work across: SQL · Python · Power BI · Generative AI · Agile · Jira · AWS

I work best with FinTech scale-ups, product-led companies, investment banks, B2B enterprise teams, Big Four and global consulting firms who need someone who can own the full problem, from discovery to delivery.

Currently completing an MSc in Data Analytics at Dublin City University (graduating Sep 2026), deepening my work in machine learning, AI and cloud technologies to build smarter, more predictive products.

Actively exploring full-time Project Manager, Product Manager and Senior Business Analyst roles where data and AI sit at the core of what gets built.
